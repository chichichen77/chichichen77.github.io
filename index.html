    International ICT Innovative Services Awards 2022

    In a fast-paced society, it isn't easy to find good friends, so this research aims to create a chatbot exclusive to the user. Unlike common chatbots or dating apps on the market, our research's chatbot, the Language Style of Chatbot, reply's sentences will be converted through the "Language Style Model" . This model is innovatively created by this research. The language style of the chatbot will be conformed to the user's preference, and the reply will instantly break the time limit. The "Language Style Model" combining N-gram, Sentence Deconstruction model, and Word2FunctionVector. The sentence deconstruction model and Word2FunctionVector were innovatively established for this research. Its functions are briefly described below:
    1.Sentence deconstruction model: Its function is to learn the language style of an author. Its operation process first is to segment the author's article into sentences and words, then convert the terms according to the part of speech, and finally record them in the model.
    2.Word2FunctionVector: Similar to the usage of Word2Vec, it can convert a set of words into a set of vector spaces to express their semantic meaning, and the vector space given to terms by Word2FunctionVector can better represent their usage. For example, the words "I" and "you" have different meanings, and their parts of speech are personal pronouns, and their use is very similar. For example, "I am delighted" and "You are pleased" both express that someone is happy, but the words of this two sentences are not the same. In this case, the vectors assigned to the two words by Word2FunctionVector will be similar verse with the vector given by Word2Vec.
    Also, our research can use the MBTI test and three-stage multiple-choice questions to customize a user-specific chatbot. The options in the three-stage multiple-choice questions are all sentences transformed by the Language Style Model. The Language Style Model improves the problem of standardized responses of chatbots on the market and better meet user preferences to create their chatbots.
